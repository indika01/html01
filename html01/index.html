About Conference

Information & Communication is playing an increasing role in society and within our lives.Recognizing this,FICC 2018 aims to provide a forum for researchers from both academia and industry to share their latest research contributions,future vision in the field and potential impact across industries.Join us,April 5-6,to explore discovery,progress and achievements related to Communication,Data science,Computing and Internet of Things.Click here for Conference topics.The conference will provide its attendees an uncommon opportunity to expand their network beyond their immediate proffessional environment.It is a unique chance to work with other accomplished individuals from diverse areas towards the common goal of shaping the future of the communication,computing and society.FICC is part of the SAI Conferences,based in the United Kingdom.Previous conferences include include Future Technologies Conference (FTC)in San Fransisco, Intelligent Systems Conference(IntelliSys)in London and Computing Conference in London.Authors are kindly invited to submit their papers/posters/demo proposals as per the schedule below,

Submission Guidelines

Papers are accepted either through the online submission tool or email at FICC@SAI Conference.com.For those unable to use the online System and are emailing,please include a cover letter with author details,university or country information,and any ohter author related data.

  • .docx/.pdf/Latex PDF formats are accepted
  • Do not include author details,university or counter information in the manuscript.This is a double-blind peer review process.Author information should be supplied using the manuscript submission form online or in the for email submissions.
  • Please ensure all articles are proofread and checked for accuracy prior to submission.Changes cannot be made during the review process.If accepted,minor revisions may be made prior to final submission.
